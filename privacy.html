Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Profix Appliances ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you contact us, request service, or use our website and messaging channels.

Information we collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including your name, phone number, email address, service address, appliance details, photos or videos of the issue, and communication preferences. This information may be collected when you:

  • Submit a request through our website.
  • Contact us via phone, text message, or email.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

We do not sell your personal information. These providers are only permitted to use your information as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

We take reasonable measures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices and rights

You may request to update, correct, or delete certain personal information that we hold about you, subject to applicable law. To do so, please contact us using any of the methods listed on our main website.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.

Contact
